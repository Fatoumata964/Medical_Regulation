E. General Information on the Trial
E.1 Medical condition or disease under investigation
E.1.1 Medical condition(s) being investigated: Severe sepsis caused by intraabdominal infection either peritonitis or pancreatitis
MedDRA Classification
E.1.3 Condition being studied is a rare disease: No
E.2 Objective of the trial
E.2.1 Main objective of the trial: To study penetration of ertapenem into abdominal cavity during severe sepsis caused by intrabdominal infection
E.2.2 Secondary objectives of the trial: To relate measured intraabdominal concentration to pharmacodynamic measure of antimicrobial efficiency: time over MIC.
E.2.3 Trial contains a sub-study: No
E.3 Principal inclusion criteria: adult aged 18-65 years
severe sepsis, according to international criteria
peritonitis and/or severe pancreatitis
E.4 Principal exclusion criteria: renal dysfunction, defined as creatinine clearance less than 30 ml/min calculated using Cockroft-Gault formula
morbidly obese patients with BMI over 35 kg/m2
E.5 End points
E.5.1 Primary end point(s): concentration of ertapenem in abdominal cavity
